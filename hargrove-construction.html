About Us

A Family Name
Hampton Trusts

Hargrove Construction Group was founded in 1997 by Robert Hargrove Sr. with a single crew, a beat-up Ford F-250, and a conviction that the Hampton Roads area deserved a contractor that didn't cut corners. That belief still drives everything we do.

Today, his son Robert Jr. leads a team of 60-plus experienced tradespeople across Hampton, Newport News, and the wider Tidewater region. Whether it's a ground-up commercial build, a full residential remodel, or an industrial project for one of the area's many defense contractors, we bring the same level of care and accountability to every job.

  • Licensed General Contractor — Commonwealth of Virginia
  • OSHA-compliant safety program, zero lost-time incidents in 2023
  • Certified Small Business with Hampton City
  • Proud member of AGC of Virginia since 2001

How We Work

No surprises.
Just a clear path forward.

01

Discovery Call

We start with a 30-minute call to understand your project scope, budget range, and timeline. No pressure, no sales pitch — just a real conversation.

02

Site Walk & Estimate

We visit the site, review any existing plans, and put together a detailed written estimate. Turnaround is typically 5–7 business days.

03

Planning & Permitting

Once contracted, we handle all permitting, schedule the trades, and provide you with a full project timeline before a single shovel hits the ground.

04

Build & Close Out

Weekly updates, a dedicated superintendent on-site daily, and a thorough punch-list walk at the end. We don't close out until you're satisfied.
