Our Story

What makes
Amy's different?

Bánh mì is more than a sandwich. It's a piece of living history — born when French baguette culture collided with Vietnamese ingenuity in the 19th century, creating something entirely new and entirely Vietnamese.

At Amy's, every loaf is baked fresh each morning. Every filling is house-made. Every bite connects you to a tradition that crossed an ocean and found a home right here on Armistead Ave.

Fresh-baked daily Real ingredients Authentic recipes Family-run
Rooted in Culture

The history
behind every bite.

Vietnam's culinary tradition spans over 4,000 years — bold broths, fresh herbs, and flavors in perfect balance. We carry that heritage proudly, right here in Hampton Roads.


The bánh mì is celebrated worldwide. Ours honors every layer of that story.
